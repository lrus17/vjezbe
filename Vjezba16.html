Seamaster Automatic Chronograph Men's Watch

Stainless steel case with a stainless steel bracelet. Uni-directional rotating silver-tone stainless steel bezel with a black ceramic top ring. Black dial with luminous skeleton hands and dot hour markers. Minute markers around the outer rim. Dial Type: Analog. Luminescent hands and markers. Date display at the 6 o'clock position. Chronograph - three sub-dials displaying 60 second, 30 minute, and 12 hour.
